About this product:

This Joomla extension displays Facebook Page (also called Fanpage) albums into your joomla website as a gallery or individual albums. Use the powerful servers of Facebook to host and administer your photos and use this extension to display them on your site.

System Requirements

  • Joomla installation with proper directory permission and JavaScript error free frontend
  • PHP 5
  • php5-curl library installed or enabled or allow_url_fopen turned on in php.ini file
