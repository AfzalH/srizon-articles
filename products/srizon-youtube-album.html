Srizon Responsive YouTube Album

About this product:

Srizon Youtube Album is a WordPress Plugin for showing your Youtube videos on your website as a gallery/album. It shows the thumbs as a preview and shows the video when the thumbs are clicked. Videos are displayed on a lightbox. You can also display the title and description of each video (layout dependent).

System Requirements

  • Wordpress installation JavaScript error free frontend
  • PHP 5
  • php5-curl library installed or enabled or allow_url_fopen turned on in php.ini file
