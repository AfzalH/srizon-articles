About this product:

JUsertube is a Joomla Extension for showing your Youtube videos on your website as a gallery/album. It shows the thumbs as a preview and shows the video when the thumbs are clicked. Videos can be displayed on a lightbox or an embedded player (in some layout). You can also display the title and description of each video (layout dependent).

System Requirements

  • Joomla installation with proper directory permission and JavaScript error free frontend
  • PHP 5
  • php5-curl library installed or enabled or allow_url_fopen turned on in php.ini file
